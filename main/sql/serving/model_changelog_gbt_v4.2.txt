Author: ML Engineering Team
Status: Production

---

SUMMARY OF CHANGES FROM v4.1 TO v4.2
--------------------------------------

1. FEATURE ENGINEERING

v4.1 used 12 input features:
- hour_of_day, day_of_week, month, is_weekend
- temperature_2m, cloud_cover, shortwave_radiation
- wind_speed_10m, precipitation
- lag_1h_energy, lag_24h_energy, lag_168h_energy

v4.2 adds 6 new features (18 total):
- direct_normal_irradiance (DNI): improves accuracy during partial cloud events
- diffuse_radiation: captures scattered light contribution
- aqi_value: air quality affects panel efficiency via soiling and transmittance
- wind_gusts_10m: accounts for panel cooling effect
- sunshine_hours: daily cumulative sunshine as a trend signal
- rolling_7d_avg_energy: smoothed energy trend over 7 days

Removed in v4.2:
- is_weekend: found to be redundant with day_of_week encoding

2. MODEL ARCHITECTURE

v4.1:
- n_estimators: 300
- max_depth: 6
- learning_rate: 0.05
- subsample: 0.8
- colsample_bytree: 0.8

v4.2:
- n_estimators: 500 (increased for new features)
- max_depth: 7
- learning_rate: 0.03 (reduced to prevent overfitting with more features)
- subsample: 0.85
- colsample_bytree: 0.75
- min_child_weight: 5 (new parameter, reduces overfitting on small facilities)

3. TRAINING DATA

v4.1 training period: 2023-01-01 to 2024-06-30 (18 months)
v4.2 training period: 2023-01-01 to 2025-01-31 (25 months)

Facilities included in training:
- Avonlie (AVLSF), 254.1 MW
- Bomen (BOMENSF), 121.0 MW
- Darlington Point (DARLSF), 324.0 MW
- Emerald (EMERASF), 88.0 MW
- Finley (FINLEYSF), 162.36 MW
- Limondale 2 (LIMOSF2), 38.0 MW
- White Rock Solar Farm (WRSF1), 22.0 MW
- Yatpool (YATSF1), 94.0 MW

Train/validation split: 80/20 with time-based split (no data leakage).

5. KNOWN LIMITATIONS

- Forecast accuracy drops to R2 = 0.79 during dust storm events (AQI > 200).
- Horizon beyond 48 hours: MAPE increases to 11.2%.
- White Rock Solar Farm has limited training samples due to plant age (commissioned 2021).
- Night-time hours (22:00-05:00) excluded from error metrics; model predicts near-zero correctly.

6. DEPLOYMENT NOTES

Model artifact: gbt_v4.2_all_facilities_2025-03-15.pkl
Serving endpoint: /api/forecast/72h
Feature pipeline: runs every 30 minutes, pulls from lh_silver_clean_hourly_weather and lh_silver_clean_hourly_air_quality
Warm-up period after deployment: 24 hours of shadow mode
Rollback trigger: if 24h MAPE exceeds 12% in production

7. COMPARISON RECOMMENDATION

For production use, v4.2 is recommended over v4.1 for all facilities.
v4.1 remains available as fallback model in model registry.
Key win: AQI feature reduced RMSE by 12% at Emerald and Yatpool specifically,
where dust events are more frequent than other sites.
