SECTION 2 — SENSOR SPECIFICATIONS
------------------------------------

2.1 Pyranometer (Solar Irradiance)
Sensor model: Kipp & Zonen CMP11
Output field: shortwave_radiation (W/m2)
Range: 0 to 2000 W/m2
Accuracy: +/- 10 W/m2 or 2% of reading
Expected daily peak: 800-1200 W/m2 (clear sky, solar noon)
Fault indicator: Reading of 0 W/m2 during energy production indicates sensor fault.
Maintenance: Annually calibrated, dome cleaned monthly.

2.2 Anemometer (Wind Speed)
Sensor model: Vaisala WMT700
Output fields: wind_speed_10m (m/s), wind_gusts_10m (m/s)
Range: 0 to 75 m/s
Accuracy: +/- 0.1 m/s
Expected range for operations: 0 to 15 m/s (above 25 m/s: storm protocol)
Maintenance: Annual inspection, bearing check every 2 years.

2.3 Thermometer (Air Temperature)
Sensor model: Vaisala HMP155
Output field: temperature_2m (degrees Celsius, measured at 2m height)
Range: -40 to +60 degrees Celsius
Accuracy: +/- 0.2 degrees Celsius
Panel efficiency note: Every 1 degree above 25C reduces panel output by 0.35-0.45%.

2.4 UV Index Sensor (White Rock only)
Sensor model: Apogee SU-100
Output fields: uv_index (0-11+), uv_index_clear_sky (theoretical maximum)
Quality check: uv_index > 11 is flagged as OUT_OF_BOUNDS in quality pipeline.

2.5 Air Quality Sensor (Emerald only)
Sensor model: Alphasense OPC-N3
Output fields: aqi_value, pm2_5, pm10, dust
AQI thresholds:
  0-50:   Good
  51-100: Moderate
  101-150: Unhealthy for sensitive groups
  151-200: Unhealthy
  201-300: Very Unhealthy
  301+:   Hazardous
Quality check: aqi_value > 200 is flagged OUT_OF_BOUNDS; energy data remains valid.

SECTION 3 — DATA QUALITY THRESHOLDS
--------------------------------------

The following conditions trigger quality flags in the Silver layer:

DAYTIME_ZERO_ENERGY
  Condition: energy_mwh = 0 during hours 07:00-17:00 local time
  Likely cause: inverter fault, grid disconnection, or emergency shutdown
  Action: Check operations log for planned maintenance

PEAK_HOUR_LOW_ENERGY
  Condition: energy_mwh < 20% of capacity between 10:00-14:00
  Likely cause: heavy cloud cover, soiling, or partial outage
  Action: Compare shortwave_radiation for the same hour

NIGHT_ENERGY
  Condition: energy_mwh > 0 between 22:00-05:00
  Likely cause: meter reading error or timestamp offset
  Action: Verify meter timestamp alignment

CLOUD_MEASUREMENT_INCONSISTENCY
  Condition: cloud_cover < 20% but shortwave_radiation < 200 W/m2
  Likely cause: localized cloud passing, sensor interference
  Action: Review 30-minute resolution data if available

EXTREME_TEMPERATURE
  Condition: temperature_2m > 45 or temperature_2m < -5
  Likely cause: sensor malfunction or genuine extreme weather
  Action: Cross-check with Bureau of Meteorology station data

EQUIPMENT_DOWNTIME
  Condition: completeness_pct < 50% for any 24-hour window
  Likely cause: inverter offline, SCADA disconnection, or network outage
  Action: Raise ticket with facility operations team

TRANSITION_HOUR_LOW_ENERGY
  Condition: energy_mwh < 10% of hourly capacity during 06:00-07:00 or 17:00-18:00
  Likely cause: panel angle and low sun elevation, normal at extremes
  Action: No action required for these hours in most cases

SECTION 4 — MAINTENANCE SCHEDULE
-----------------------------------

Monthly tasks:
- Clean pyranometer domes at all facilities
- Inspect anemometer mounts for damage
- Review quality flag counts: alert if EQUIPMENT_DOWNTIME exceeds 3 events per month

Quarterly tasks:
- Panel cleaning at Avonlie, Bomen, White Rock, Limondale 2, Finley
- Full inverter diagnostics at Darlington Point and Bomen (oldest equipment)
- Download and archive inverter event logs

Annual tasks:
- Pyranometer calibration against secondary standard at all facilities
- Thermometer verification
- Panel IV-curve testing: identify degraded strings
- Expected annual degradation: 0.4-0.6% per year (industry standard)
